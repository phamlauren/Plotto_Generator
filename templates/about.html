About the project
This project was created by Lauren Pham for CLEN-GU4728: Literature in the Age of Artificial Intelligence taught by Professor Dennis Y. Tenen in the Department of English and Comparative Literature at Columbia University.
Lauren is a BA candidate for the Class of 2021 in the Department of Computer Science at Columbia University.
The project's design is inspired by Taper, an online literary magazine for small computational pieces published by Bad Quarto.
Contact her with comments or concerns at lyp2106(at)columbia(dot)edu.
